# Front Desk Check-In & VIP Arrival Procedure

**Company:** Harbor Hotel Group | **Doc ID:** FOH-CHK-011 | **Version:** 2.3 | **Effective:** 2026-02-01 | **Owner:** Front Office Operations

## Purpose & scope

Standard for front desk agents at Harbor Hotel Group properties during standard check-in, early arrival, late arrival, and VIP / loyalty tier arrivals (Harbor Gold and Harbor Platinum).

## Key terms

- **Pre-reg:** Reservation confirmed; room assigned; key packets prepared before guest arrival.
- **Upsell moment:** After confirming reservation — offer upgrade only if inventory and rate rules allow.
- **VIP trace:** CRM notes on preferences (pillow type, minibar, newspaper, allergy).

## Standard procedure

1. Acknowledge guest within 20 seconds of approaching desk — eye contact and welcome by property name.
2. Request reservation name and photo ID; locate folio in PMS (HarborOne).
3. Confirm dates, room type, rate; read back total and incidentals hold amount.
4. For VIP tiers: acknowledge status by name ("Welcome back, Ms. Chen — Platinum member"); confirm VIP trace items.
5. Explain key cards, Wi-Fi, breakfast hours, gym location — max 45 seconds unless guest asks more.
6. Bell staff offer for luggage; escort to elevator for Platinum if policy active.

## Approved phrases

> "Good evening and welcome to Harbor Hotel Boston. May I have the name on the reservation?"

> "I've upgraded you to a harbor-view king as a Platinum benefit — your keys are ready."

> "We have a package waiting for you — I'll have it sent to your room within ten minutes."

> "Checkout is at eleven, but I can note a late checkout subject to availability tomorrow morning."

## Scenarios

**Scenario A — Early arrival, room not ready:** Guest arrives 9 AM; room dirty. Agent stores luggage, offers lobby coffee voucher, texts when room ready; never quotes exact time unless housekeeping confirms.

**Scenario B — Overbooking:** Walk-in with confirmed reservation; no room clean. Agent apologizes, offers partner hotel transport + comp night certificate per policy OB-001; manager approval required.

**Scenario C — VIP allergy trace:** Platinum guest with nut allergy noted. Agent confirms kitchen alert, removes minibar items with nuts before key handoff.

## Escalation matrix

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Rate dispute | Duty manager |
| Security concern (fake ID) | Manager + security |
| Guest injury in lobby | Incident report + GM notification |
| Media / celebrity | GM pre-approved privacy protocol |

## Do / Don't

**Do:** Stand when guest approaches; use guest name three times during interaction; smile in phone voice for call-ahead arrivals.

**Don't:** Discuss room numbers aloud when other guests wait; promise upgrades without system authorization; share other guests' stay information.
