# Parler Bien — 90-Second Sales Pitch Demo Script

**Total runtime:** ~90 seconds · **Audience:** RAISE Summit judges

## Pre-flight

- Open `/demo` or tap **Hackathon Demo** on the home screen.
- Confirm mic permissions and `OPENROUTER_API_KEY` in `.env.local`.

---

## Beat 0 — Hook (10s)

**Say:**

> "Your pitch fails in the first ten seconds — before your slide loads."

**Do:**

1. Point at the **weak delivery** card (filler words, uptalk).
2. Point at the **strong delivery** card (warm, brief, permission-based open).

---

## Beat 1 — Cold open (20s)

**Say:**

> "Same script, two deliveries — watch the score move."

**Do:**

1. Pre-loaded: **"Hi Sarah — this is Alex. Did I catch you at a bad time?"**
2. **Take 1:** Flat, rushed, add "um" and "just" — show low delivery score.
3. **Take 2:** Warm pause, downward inflection — score jump + coach tip.

**Second-take tip:**

> "Drop filler — smile through the open, pause after their name."

---

## Beat 2 — Objection + reframe (25s)

**Say:**

> "Objections aren't about words — it's whether you sound defensive or credible."

**Do:**

1. Pre-loaded objection line about CRM vs delivery.
2. **Take 1:** Fast, argumentative delivery.
3. **Take 2:** Empathy first, calm reframe — highlight word chips on "hear you" / "gap".

**Second-take tip:**

> "Empathy first, reframe second — end statements down, not up."

---

## Beat 3 — Market vendor (25s)

**Say:**

> "Voice is leverage — same words, confident delivery closes the deal."

**Do:**

1. French switches on — **"Vingt euros, et on conclut?"**
2. Show **Deal likelihood** meter climbing on confident take.
3. Character TTS reacts — pronunciation + negotiation in one beat.

**Second-take tip:**

> "Lower register, clear ending — confidence beats vocabulary."

---

## Close (15s)

**Say:**

> "Parler Bien scores sales delivery — opens, objections, and closes — so reps sound credible before the live call."

**Do:** Flash trainer grid → back to home.

---

## Exact starter lines

```
Cold open: Hi Sarah — this is Alex. Did I catch you at a bad time?

Objection: I hear you — most teams already have a CRM. The gap isn't the script, it's delivery under pressure.

Close (FR): Vingt euros, et on conclut?
```
